Check-in and Check-out Policy

Late check-outs

Late check-outs need to be coordinated with the front desk a day in advance and will be confirmed upon availability.
Check out between 11:00 - 15:00 requires an additional payment of $25. Check outs after 15:00 will be charged a full night.
If you have a late departure, you may leave your luggage next to the front desk until you leave.

Section 3 of the Hotel Keepers Act

From and after the coming into operation of this Act, no hotel keeper shall be liable to make good to any guest of such hotel keeper any loss of, or injury to, goods brought to his hotel, to a greater amount than the sum of four hundred dollars, except:

  1. where such goods are stolen, lost or injured through the wilful act, default or negligence of such hotel keeper, or any servant in his employ; or
  2. where such goods are deposited expressly for safe custody with such hotel keeper: Provided that in the case of such deposit it shall be a condition precedent of the liability of the hotel keeper that the guest affecting the deposit shall at the time thereof:
    1. make and sign a written declaration as to the nature and value of the goods deposited;
    2. permit the hotel keeper by himself, his servant or agent to inspect such goods; and
    3. if so required by the hotel keeper, deposit such goods in a box or other receptacle fastened and sealed by such guest.
